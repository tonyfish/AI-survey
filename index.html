Beyond the Diamond: The Limits of V1

The Diamond framework you just completed helped locate you on the spectrum between inevitability and agency, between dystopia and utopia. It revealed why AI governance debates feel intractable‚Äîparticipants operate from fundamentally different premises about control and outcomes.

But the Diamond assumes a world simpler than the one we inhabit.

What the Diamond Misses

  • AI is not monolithic: We're witnessing fragmentation across multiple layers‚Äîfrontier models, organizational AI systems, infrastructural intelligence, and agentic architectures‚Äîeach evolving at different speeds under different incentives.
  • Governance and markets aren't opposing forces: They're locked in dynamic feedback loops, each continuously reshaping the other.
  • The moral psychology layer is missing: Questions of trust, accountability, and stewardship determine whether coordination matures or collapses.
  • "AI development" ‚â† "frontier model race": Most real-world consequences (and ROI) are being decided through organizational and architectural work.
